Instruction for Authors

    IEEE PEDES 2024 invites researchers, practitioners, and students worldwide to submit previously unpublished original papers for consideration to be presented at the conference. All submissions will be peer reviewed.

Digest Submission Guidelines

  • Initial submission of digest (4 page max.) including figures and references are allowed in pdf format.
  • Total file size of the digest must not exceed 2 MB.
  • Accepted papers after the initial review will get the opportunity to submit full paper (6 pages max.).
  • Up to two (2) extra pages are permissible with an additional fee to be paid at the time of registration.

All digests must be written in English with all the key contributions highlighted.

Initial submission of digest (4 page max.) including figures and references are allowed in pdf format. Total file size of the digest must not exceed 2 MB.

  • Accepted papers after the initial review will get the opportunity to submit full paper (5 pages max.)
  • Up to two (2) extra pages are permissible with an additional fee to be paid at the time of registration.

Please note that

1. All digests submitted to PEDES 2022 will go through a plagiarism check. Digests with similarity scores of 30% or above are liable to be rejected without further review.

2. Acceptance of the digest does not necessarily imply acceptance of the final paper.

The organizing committee of PEDES 2022 reserves the right to reject papers for the following reasons (one or more of):

  • With poor language
  • With poor technical content
  • Not addressing reviewer's remarks
  • Dissimilarity between contents of digest and final uploaded manuscript.
