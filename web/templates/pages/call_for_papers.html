INSTRUCTIONS FOR AUTHORS

  • IEEE PEDES 2024 invites researchers, practitioners, and students worldwide to submit previously unpublished original papers for consideration to be presented at the conference. All submissions will be peer-reviewed.
  • Templates for Digest Submission: MSWord | Latex
  • Please note that the submission site does not send automatic emails of acknowledgment. Authors may, however, use the icon named "send email" available towards the end of the submission process near the top right side corner of the screen to get the submission site to send a confirmation email to themselves.

DIGEST SUBMISSION GUIDELINES

  • Initial submission of digest (4 pages max.) including figures and references are allowed in PDF format.
  • Total file size of the digest must not exceed 2 MB.
  • Accepted papers after the initial review will get the opportunity to submit a full paper (6 pages max.).
  • Up to two (2) extra pages are permissible with an additional fee to be paid at the time of registration.

IMPORTANT DEADLINES FOR AUTHORS

  • Opening Date of Submission of Digest: 15th February 2024
  • Last Date of Submission of Digest: 31st May 2024
  • Acceptance Notification of Digest: 15th August 2024
  • Submission of Final Manuscript and Copyright Form: 1st September 2024
  • Conference Registration Deadlline: 5th December 2024

IMPORTANT NOTICE

1. All digests submitted to PEDES 2024 will undergo a plagiarism check. Digests with similarity scores of 30% or above may be rejected without further review.

2. Acceptance of the digest does not guarantee acceptance of the final paper.

The organizing committee of PEDES 2024 reserves the right to reject papers for the following reasons (one or more of):

  • With poor language
  • With poor technical content
  • Not addressing reviewer's remarks
  • Dissimilarity between contents of digest and final uploaded manuscript.

We hope PEDES 2024 will be a remarkable and unforgettable event for you.
