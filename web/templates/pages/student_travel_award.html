Student Travel Award

We are excited to announce the Student Travel Awards for IEEE PEDES 2024, to be held on 18-21 December 2024 at the National Institute of Technology Karnataka (NITK) Surathkal.

Professor Som Nath Mahendra Memorial Student Travel Awards
These student travel awards will be given to up to 5 recipients in memory of the late Prof. Som Nath Mahendra from IIT (BHU), Varanasi, India. The award consists of a certificate and 10,000 INR to cover registration and travel expenses.

Prof. Subhashish Nandi Memorial Student Travel Awards
These student travel awards will be given to up to 5 recipients in memory of the late Prof. Subhashish Nandi from the University of Victoria, BC, Canada. The award consists of a certificate and 10,000 INR to cover registration and travel expenses.

North East India Student Travel Awards
These student travel awards will be given to up to 10 recipients enrolled full-time at the engineering institutes/Universities in north-east India. The award consists of a certificate and 30,000 INR to cover registration and travel expenses.

More information will be available in July 2024.
