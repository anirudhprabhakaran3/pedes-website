IEEE PEDES 2024 Conference

    IEEE PEDES 2024 invites researchers, practitioners, and students worldwide to submit previously unpublished original papers for consideration to be presented at the conference. All submissions will be peer-reviewed.

Template paper submission: Link(To be updated)

Please note that the submission site does not send automatic emails of acknowledgment. Authors may use the button named "Email" which will be available towards the end of the submission process near the top right side corner of the screen to send a confirmation email to all authors.

Manuscripts must be written in English.

The maximum number of pages including figures and references allowed in PDF format is four (4). Total file size of the digest must not exceed 2 MB. Up to two (2) extra pages are permissible, with an additional fee to be paid at the time of registration.

Important Notes:

  • All manuscripts submitted to PEDES 2024 will go through a plagiarism check. Digests with a similarity score of 30% or above are liable to be rejected without further review.
  • The acceptance of the paper does not necessarily imply acceptance of the final camera-ready paper in the conference proceedings or in IEEEXplore. The submitted camera-ready paper will be checked for further compliance by the conference publication committee.
  • Online presentation will be permitted for foreign authors.

Reasons for Rejection:

  • Poor language
  • Poor technical content
  • Not addressing reviewer's remarks
  • High similarity index
  • Deviations from IEEE conference template

We hope PEDES 2024 will be one of the remarkable and unforgettable events for you.
